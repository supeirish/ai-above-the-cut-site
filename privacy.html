Privacy

AI Above the Cut is an independent email publication. The short version: we collect the minimum needed to send you the newsletter, we don't sell your data, and you can leave at any time.

Last updated: July 16, 2026

What we collect

Your email address when you subscribe; optional details you choose to give us; engagement data from the emails we send (whether a message was opened and which links were clicked), used only to understand what's useful; and basic, aggregate site analytics such as page views. We do not use analytics to identify you individually.

How we use it

To send you the weekly brief and occasional publication updates, and to measure and improve the newsletter. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

Who processes it

We use MailerLite as our email service provider to store subscriber information and deliver emails, as a data processor acting on our behalf under its own privacy and security terms. Our website is hosted on Netlify, which may process standard technical data (such as IP address and request logs) as part of serving the site. We do not run third-party advertising or cross-site tracking. We share your information with no one else except as required by law.

Your choices

Unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email; removal is immediate. Manage your preferences through the link in any email. Access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you — just email us.

Data retention

We keep your subscriber information for as long as you're subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we retain only what's needed to honor your opt-out and meet legal obligations.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@aiabovethecut.com.
